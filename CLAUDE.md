## Design Context

### Users
Mixed audience: from sole traders (JDG) and freelancers to established SMEs with 10-50+ employees. They arrive looking for a trustworthy accounting partner — often stressed about compliance, taxes, or switching from a previous firm. They need to feel safe handing over their financials within seconds of landing on the page. The site is in Polish, targeting Warsaw-area businesses.

### Brand Personality
**Warm, trustworthy, grounded.** Not a faceless corporation, not a hip startup — a reliable partner that happens to operate under the patronage of Fundacja Donandi. The tone is human and reassuring: "We've got this handled so you can focus on your business." Three words: **ciepły, solidny, przejrzysty** (warm, solid, transparent).

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light mode — accounting clients browse during business hours, on desktops and phones, looking for clarity and reassurance. Light surfaces signal openness.
- **References**: infakt.pl and wfirma.pl — both admired for feeling modern and clean. Key takeaways: generous whitespace, clear typographic hierarchy, card-based feature/pricing layouts, strong single-color CTAs, professional but not cold.
- **Anti-references**: Generic Polish "biuro rachunkowe" websites with stock handshake photos, blue gradients, and cramped layouts. Also avoid: glassmorphism-heavy SaaS aesthetic, dark mode with neon accents, overly playful/startup energy.
- **Differentiation**: The Fundacja Donandi connection is unique — lean into the foundation's mission and values as a trust signal. This isn't just another accounting firm; it has purpose behind it.

### Design Principles
1. **Trust at first glance** — Every design decision should reinforce credibility. Real content over decoration. Clear structure over visual noise.
2. **Warmth without softness** — Approachable and human, but never cutesy or casual. This is people's money — treat it with appropriate gravity.
3. **Clarity over cleverness** — Polish-language audience scanning for services and pricing. Make information findable in under 3 seconds. No mystery meat navigation.
4. **Breathing room** — Generous whitespace, clear section boundaries, readable line lengths. Let the content speak without competing for attention.
5. **Purposeful restraint** — Animations and effects should feel intentional, not performative. One well-timed entrance beats ten hover effects.
